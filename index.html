My country

And now, probably my favorite part, my country MACEDONIA. I have mixed feelings when writing for this part, because it can be my favorite and my least favorite part to write about at the same time. I live in Macedonia, in small place called Rosoman. Our country gained it's freedom in 1991 through voting, unlike the other countries from former Yugoslavia which had to go through war to gain their independence. I'm nationalist and not patriot, because a lot of people don't know the difference between patriot and nationalist, I'll try to explain the difference. Patriot is someone who loves the country, the culture maybe, nationalist is someone who loves the nation, the Macedonian culture, the Macedonian people, language, so, for me as a nationalist, Macedonia will exist as long as there's at least one Macedonian alive. My country has been robbed and betrayed by the politicians many times, corruption is high to the sky, but I still have faith that one day, we, the true Macedonians will raise this country from the ashes like the beautiful bird, the Fenix.
